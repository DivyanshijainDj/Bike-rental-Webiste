About us

Welcome to Bikebook

Rideit is a bike rental platform which aims to fill the void in the transportation sector. The platform is designed primarily for daily commuters who rely on public transportation. It allows users to choose from a variety of bikes available at affordable prices and promises to deliver quality service.

Rideit aims to solve the absence of last and first-mile connectivity that exists in most of the major cities in India. Rideit provides the users with the experience of a personalised ride where the process of renting a bike is made easy and hassle-free.
